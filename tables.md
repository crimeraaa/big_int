# Addition

## Binary

|    | 00 | 01 |
|----|----|----|
| 00 | 00 | 01 |
| 01 | 01 | 10 |


```
  0b1101    (13)
+ 0b0010    (02)
----------------
       1    (01)
      1     (02)
     1      (04)
    1       (08)
----------------
= 0b1111    (15)

================

  0b0010    (02)
+ 0b0011    (03)
----------------
       1    (01)
     10     (04)
----------------
= 0b0101    (05)

================

  0b0010    (02)
+ 0b0111    (07)
----------------
       1    (01)
     10     (04)
     1      (04)
----------------
= 0b1001    (09)
```

# Multiplication

## Binary

|    | 00 | 01 |
|----|----|----|
| 00 | 00 | 00 |
| 01 | 00 | 01 |

```
  0b0000_1101   (13)
* 0b0000_0010   (02)
--------------------
         0000   (00)
       1_101    (26)
      00_00     (00)
     000_0      (00)
--------------------
= 0b0001_1010   (26)

====================  

  0b0000_1001   (09)
* 0b0000_1001   (09)
--------------------
         1001   (09)
       0_000    (00)
      00_00     (00)
     100_1      (72)
--------------------
= 0b0101_0001   (81)
```
